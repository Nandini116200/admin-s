How to read these

Growth tells you whether the top of the funnel is filling faster than the bottom drains. Watch for new subscribers to outpace cancellations every day.

Operations is the promise you keep with customers. On-time delivery and first-attempt success directly drive retention.

Revenue quality shows how predictable the business is. A high subscription-revenue share means smoother cash flow.

Retention is where dairy brands win or lose. Every 0.5% drop in churn compounds into a much larger active base.
